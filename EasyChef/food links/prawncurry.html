Prawn Curry

Process:

  1. Clean and devein 500g of prawns. Set them aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add 1 chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add 2 chopped tomatoes, 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1 teaspoon of coriander powder, 1/2 teaspoon of cumin powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  5. Cook the masala mixture for a few minutes until the tomatoes are soft and the spices are well combined.
  6. Add the cleaned prawns to the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes until they turn pink and are cooked through.
  7. Add 1 cup of coconut milk and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot Prawn Curry with steamed rice or naan bread.
