Chicken 65

Process:

  1. In a bowl, marinate 500g of boneless chicken pieces with 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste, 1 tablespoon of red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of garam masala, 1 tablespoon of cornflour, 1 tablespoon of all-purpose flour, 1 tablespoon of yogurt, and salt to taste. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. In a deep pan or wok, heat oil for deep frying over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken pieces in small batches and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove them using a slotted spoon and drain on a paper towel to remove excess oil.
  4. In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add 1 tablespoon of finely chopped garlic, 1 tablespoon of finely chopped ginger, and 2-3 green chilies (slit). Saut√© until aromatic.
  5. Add curry leaves and stir-fry for a few seconds.
  6. Add 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper powder, 1/2 teaspoon of cumin powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  7. Add the fried chicken pieces and toss them in the spice mixture until evenly coated.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot as an appetizer or side dish.
