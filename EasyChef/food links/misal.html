Misal

Process:

  1. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add 2 teaspoons of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add 2 chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of Misal masala, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  5. Add 2 cups of sprouted moth beans (matki) and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil. Add 1/2 teaspoon of mustard seeds, 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  7. Add a pinch of asafoetida, 8-10 curry leaves, and 2-3 dried red chilies. Fry for a few seconds.
  8. Add the tempering to the prepared Misal and mix well.
  9. For serving, place a portion of Misal in a bowl. Top it with finely chopped onions, coriander leaves, farsan (fried savory snack), and sev (crispy chickpea flour noodles).
  10. Serve hot Misal with pav (bread rolls) and enjoy!
