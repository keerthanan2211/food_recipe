Chicken Patties

Process:

  1. In a bowl, combine 500g minced chicken, 1 finely chopped onion, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1/4 cup breadcrumbs, 1 beaten egg, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1 teaspoon paprika, salt, and pepper to taste. Mix well.
  2. Divide the mixture into equal-sized portions and shape them into patties. Set them aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Cook the chicken patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until cooked through and golden brown.
  4. Remove the patties from the pan and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  5. Serve the Chicken Patties hot as a snack or in burger buns with your favorite toppings and condiments.
