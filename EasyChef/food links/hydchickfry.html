Hyderabadi Chicken Fry

Process:

  1. Clean and wash 500g of chicken pieces. Pat them dry and set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add 2 tablespoons of ginger-garlic paste, 1 tablespoon of red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 tablespoon of coriander powder, 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, and salt to taste. Mix well to form a marinade.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them well. Let the chicken marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  4. In a deep pan or skillet, heat oil for frying. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken pieces in batches and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove the fried chicken pieces from the pan and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot Hyderabadi Chicken Fry as an appetizer or side dish.
