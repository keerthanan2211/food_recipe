Mushroom Curry

Process:

  1. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee.
  2. Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until it turns golden brown.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add 2 chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1 teaspoon of coriander powder, 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  6. Add 200 grams of mushrooms (cleaned and sliced) and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add 1/2 cup of water and simmer for another 5 minutes until the mushrooms are tender.
  8. Add 1/4 cup of coconut milk and cook for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot Mushroom Curry with rice, roti, or naan.
