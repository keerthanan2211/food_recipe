Mutter Paneer

Process:

  1. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee. Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until it turns golden brown.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add 2 finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1 teaspoon of coriander powder, 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  6. Add 1 cup of green peas (mutter) and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add 200 grams of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cubes and mix gently to coat them with the masala.
  8. Cook for another 5 minutes until the paneer is heated through.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot Mutter Paneer with naan, roti, or rice.
