Chana Masala

Process:

  1. Soak 1 cup of chickpeas (chana) in water overnight. Drain the water.
  2. In a pressure cooker or large pot, add the soaked chickpeas and enough water to cover them. Cook for about 20-25 minutes until the chickpeas are tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee.
  4. Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until it turns translucent.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  6. Add 2 finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  7. Add 2 teaspoons of chana masala powder, 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of cumin powder, 1 teaspoon of coriander powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  8. Add the cooked chickpeas and mix well to coat them with the spice mixture. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  9. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  10. Serve hot Chana Masala with rice, roti, or naan.
