Butter Chicken

Process:

  1. Marinate 500g of boneless chicken pieces with 1 cup of yogurt, 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of garam masala, 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, and salt to taste. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  2. In a large pan, heat 2 tablespoons of butter or oil. Add 1 diced onion and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add 2 diced tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and let the mixture cool. Then, blend it into a smooth paste.
  6. In the same pan, heat 1 tablespoon of butter or oil. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is browned and cooked through.
  7. Add the tomato paste, 1 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1 teaspoon of garam masala, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  8. Add 1 cup of heavy cream and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a dollop of butter.
  10. Serve hot Butter Chicken with naan or rice.
