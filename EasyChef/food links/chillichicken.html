Chilli Chicken

Process:

  1. In a bowl, marinate 500g of boneless chicken pieces with 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of vinegar, 1 teaspoon of ginger paste, 1 teaspoon of garlic paste, salt to taste, and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper powder. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. In a deep pan or wok, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of finely chopped garlic and saut√© until aromatic.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of finely chopped green chilies and stir-fry for a minute.
  5. Add 1 sliced onion and cook until it turns translucent.
  6. Add 1 sliced bell pepper (capsicum) and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  8. In a small bowl, mix 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of tomato ketchup, 1 tablespoon of chili sauce, and 1/2 teaspoon of sugar. Pour this sauce mixture over the cooked chicken and vegetables.
  9. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring well to coat the chicken and vegetables with the sauce.
  10. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
